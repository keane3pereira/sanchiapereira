Rekai Service Design Case Competition
1. Skills Gained Service Design Process Mastery, Empathy Interviews, Problem Framing, Field Research using POEMS (People, Objects, Environment, Messages, Services) Method
2. The Project The Service Design case competition is a 4 week long collaboration with the Business Design, Healthcare Management and Pride Clubs. The client for 2023 was a long term care home, where we developing a solution for their Pride wing
3. The Challenge
4. Design Process
5. Secondary Research We kickstarted the project by doing desk research about the long term care home & rainbow wing from the kickoff, website, annual report, news articles, to youtube videos, we formulated our business problem hypothesis which is that the Centre is proud to be the first long-term care home with a unit dedicated to pride residents and wants to continue it, and be remembered that way, and from there, we assumed that the design problem we had to dive into was that Rainbow wing lacks the interactions & outlets to pass down pride community's rich history from the current to future rainbow wing residents.
6. Field Research: Observations We were the only team who visited the center in person and observed the surroundings.
7. POEMS Method Sorted the information from the empathy interviews.
8. Empathy Interviews There were 3 panel sessions conducted virtually which were moderated by the Healthcare Management Association's president from Rotman using the teams' submitted questions. Due to time constraints and sensitivity of the subject, the moderator asked the pre-approved submitted questions and there was no Q&A.
